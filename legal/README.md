## Key Legal Protections

### What These Documents Cover:

1. **No Guarantees Clause**
   - Clear statement that no profits are guaranteed
   - Past performance ≠ future results
   - Risk of total loss

2. **Limitation of Liability**
   - Not responsible for betting losses
   - Not responsible for technical errors
   - Maximum liability = last monthly fee

3. **User Responsibilities**
   - 18+ age requirement
   - Comply with local gambling laws
   - Responsible for own betting decisions
   - No sharing of predictions

4. **Subscription Terms**
   - Non-refundable payments
   - Monthly billing in advance
   - Can cancel anytime (effective end of period)

5. **Intellectual Property**
   - All predictions are copyrighted
   - No redistribution allowed
   - Violation = subscription termination

6. **Privacy & Data**
   - What data we collect (Telegram ID, username)
   - How we use it (send predictions, billing)
   - No selling of personal data
   - GDPR compliant
